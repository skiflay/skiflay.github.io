About Me

Simon Kiflay holds masters in Library and Information Science from University of Nairobi and
special certificate in Public Policy from UC Berkeley, California. Simon worked as information
manager in Research and Documentation Center of Eritrea for 6 years. After moving to USA,
he worked as MS SQL and BI Developer in different companies for more than 4 years.

Simon believes providing right information to the right person at the right time can improve
our day-to-day decision-making process and bring us all to a relatively closer perspective and
understanding of issues as a community. Currently, Simon is studying his Masters Degree in
Software Development at Maharishi International University (MIU) in Iowa.
